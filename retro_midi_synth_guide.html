Legend:   MT-32 = Roland MT-32 (1987)  |  MT-32 Old = First-gen MT-32 required (exploits firmware bugs)  |  CM-32L = Roland CM-32L or CM-64 (extra PCM sounds)  |  SC-55 = Roland Sound Canvas SC-55 / General MIDI  |  SC-88 = Roland Sound Canvas SC-88 (natively)  |  SC-88 Pro = Roland SC-88 Pro (natively)  |  OPL2 = Yamaha OPL2 / AdLib / Sound Blaster  |  OPL3 = Yamaha OPL3 / Sound Blaster 16+  |  AWE32 = Creative AWE32/AWE64 wavetable  |  GUS = Gravis UltraSound  |  XG = Yamaha XG (MU-series / DB50XG / YMF7xx)

Key principle: "Ideal synth" = the hardware the composer used or the device confirmed by community testing to sound closest to original intent. A game supporting SC-55 can still be played on OPL — it just sounds different. MT-32 and SC-55 are NOT interchangeable standards.

Modern emulation options: MT-32 → Munt emulator or MT-32 Pi  |  SC-55 → Nuked-SC55 (cycle-accurate) at github.com/jcmoyer/Nuked-SC55  |  SC-55/SC-88 → Roland Sound Canvas VA (VST plugin, requires SC-8820 basis)  |  OPL → DOSBox built-in or Nuked-OPL3  |  XG → Yamaha S-YXG50 softsynth
